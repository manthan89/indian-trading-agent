# 📊 Indian Trading Agent — How to Use

**Simple Guide for Beginners**

---

## What is this?

This is your personal AI-powered trading assistant for Indian stock markets (NSE/BSE). It helps you:
- Find the best stocks to buy today
- Check market trends
- Plan your trades
- Track paper trades (practice, no real money)

---

## How to Open

**On your phone or laptop:**

1. Make sure **Tailscale VPN is installed and running**
2. Open your browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
3. Go to: **http://100.67.246.96:3001**
4. Wait 10-15 seconds for data to load

That's it! You'll see the dashboard.

## How to Find Trades

### Step 1: See Top Picks (FREE)
1. Click **"Top Picks"** in the left menu
2. You'll see a list of stocks ranked by score
3. Stocks with **"STRONG BUY"** are the best ones
4. Each shows: Price, Change %, Confidence, Success %

### Step 2: Deep Analysis (Rs.15-25)
1. Click **"Deep Analysis"** in the menu
2. Enter a stock name (e.g., RELIANCE, TCS, INFY)
3. Click **"Run Analysis"**
4. Wait 2-3 minutes
5. Get: Entry price, Stop loss, Target price, Reasoning

### Step 3: Track Paper Trades (FREE)
1. On any stock you like, click **"Track"**
2. It creates a practice trade at current price
3. Come back later to check if it went up or down
4. No real money involved!

## Troubleshooting

**"Cannot connect to backend"**
- Wait 10 seconds and refresh
- Check Tailscale is connected

**"Loading..." forever**
- Pull down to refresh
- Check internet connection

**Data shows "0" or empty**
- Market might be closed (Indian markets close at 3:30 PM IST)
- Try during market hours (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST, Mon-Fri)
